iwr -useb get.scoop.sh | iex  
也可將程序安裝到指定目錄，如F:\GlobalScoopApps:  
```powershell
[environment]::setEnvironmentVariable(‘SCOOP_GLOBAL‘,‘F:\GlobalScoopApps‘,‘Machine‘)  
$env:SCOOP_GLOBAL=‘F:\GlobalScoopApps‘
scoop install -g <app>
```
